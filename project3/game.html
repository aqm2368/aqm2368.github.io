FLOATERS

Simple ships that drift through a fixed area of space and will change their direction of velocity instantaneously once they reach the edge.

SPINNERS

Advanced ships that can spin fast enough to create a wormhole and teleport themselves through space, typically disappearing in one direction to appear in the opposite.

SEEKERS

Hostile ships that will constantly advance towards you.

Because these ships are designed for pursuit, they can easily dodge asteroids.

ASTEROIDS

Large rocks that are unbreakable for your equipment.

Their sheer mass is capable of obliterating most ships that come into their path.
